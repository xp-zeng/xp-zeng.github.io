About me

I received my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Heilongjiang Province Key Laboratory of Space-Air-Ground Integrated Intelligent Remote Sensing (SIRS) at Harbin Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Yanfeng Gu.

My research interests include:

  • 2D Computer Vision, including detection and segmentation.

  • Video Understanding. Focus on the utilization of temporal information.

  • Interdisciplinary Studies + Deep Learning. Using Deep Learning to solve problems in other fileds (e.g., remote sensing, medial science, biology)
